Video poker (5 card draw)

  • Open console to play

Rules:

  • Start with 5 tokens
  • Pay 1 token per game
  • 5 cards dealt
  • Discard 0-5 cards
  • Discarded cards replaced
  • Receive tokens based upon hand ranking
  • Continue playing with at least 1 token

Hand ranking/payout:

  • Royal flush - Payout: 250
  • Straight flush - Payout: 50
  • Four of a kind - Payout: 25
  • Full house - Payout: 6
  • Flush - Payout: 5
  • Straight - Payout: 4
  • Three of a kind - Payout: 3
  • Two pairs - Payout: 2
  • One pair - Payout: 1
  • High card - Payout: 0
